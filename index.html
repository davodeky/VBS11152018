Sunday's Sermon

Bible Reading
  1. 1 Thessalonians 5:12-22
Sermon Summary

  • We need to be a thankful people.
  • We should be the most appreciating people- for God loved us so much that He gave His only begotten son.
  • Everybody wants to be appreciated.
  • Appreciation comes out of love.
  • We cannot show appreciation to those around us without loving them.
  • We sometimes try to show appreciation based on what we see on the outside- example of Samuel and Jesse.
  • Example of the 10 lepers
